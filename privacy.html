Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Welcome to Brainvita Master 3D. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy outlines how we handle information in our mobile and web applications.

1. Information We Collect

Brainvita Master 3D is designed as a "No Data Collection" application. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as your name, email address, location, or device identifiers.

2. Permissions

This app does not require or request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or photo library. It operates entirely locally on your device.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media integration that tracks user behavior.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any personal information, our app is safe for children of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

5. Local Storage

The app may use local storage (on your own device) to save your high scores or game preferences. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the app store listing.
