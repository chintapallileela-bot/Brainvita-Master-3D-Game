Experience the ultimate version of the classic Peg Solitaire (Brainvita) board game! Brainvita Master 3D brings the timeless puzzle to life with beautiful 3D graphics, immersive environments, and intuitive touch controls.

Whether you're a puzzle master or a casual player, Brainvita Master 3D offers a relaxing yet stimulating challenge. The goal is simple: jump over marbles to remove them until only one remains. Can you reach the center hole?

- TRACK YOUR PROGRESS: Integrated timer and collection tray for removed marbles.
- PURE GAMEPLAY: No data collection, works 100% offline.

How to Play:
1. Select a marble that has an empty hole two spaces away.
2. Jump over a neighboring marble into that hole.
3. The jumped marble is removed.
4. Keep going until no more moves are possible.
5. Win by leaving just one marble on the board!
